Avurudu Joy at Munidasa Kumarathunga Vidyalaya – Primary Section

Posted on April 20, 2025

The Avurudu celebration held on April 10th at Munidasa Kumarathunga Vidyalaya's Primary Section was a heartwarming and colorful event filled with laughter, tradition, and joy. Young students embraced the spirit of the New Year with vibrant attire, traditional games, and cultural performances that brought the school grounds to life.

As the official photographer, I had the privilege of capturing these precious moments — from the cheerful chaos of lime-and-spoon races to the graceful Avurudu Kumari competition. Each photograph tells a story of excitement, innocence, and community spirit. The shoot was a smooth and fulfilling experience, with every child bringing genuine energy to the frame. It was a truly successful day, both in celebration and in capturing memories that will be cherished for years to come.
